Built for South Africa

Compliance and infrastructure you can rely on.

StayOS is engineered around South African regulatory requirements — not retrofitted from a global platform. POPIA, NLTA, NSFAS, and local payment gateways are first-class citizens, not add-ons.

Explore the Platform
POPIA Compliant
AES-256-CBC field-level encryption for all PII. DSAR export, right to erasure, and 5-year data retention built into the data layer.
PayFast & Ozow Payments
South African payment gateways first. PayFast ITN verification, Ozow SHA-512 hash verification, and instant EFT — with Stripe for international overflow.
NSFAS & Bursary Billing
Structured student invoicing for NSFAS-funded, bursary, and self-paying students. NSFAS claim pack generation, semester billing cycles, and instalment plans.
Africa's Talking SMS
Local SMS delivery via Africa's Talking for all ZA numbers, with Twilio as international fallback. No dependency on US-first infrastructure.
